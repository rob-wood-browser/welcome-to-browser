Welcome to Browser

It's brilliant to have you on board.

We're a small team of creative people who make digital products for workplaces.

We're excited for you to join us in making clever things for good organisations. To de-stress the experience of starting a new job, a few of us have made this page to help you to get you up to speed on your first few days and weeks. Below, you'll find stuff to read, people to meet and places to go.

People

To get an idea of who's who, head to the people picker on Twine. Here you'll find who does what and an outline of their skills.

You can also take a look at our org chart to see how Browser is structured.

Places to go

We're in a great spot right in the middle of Shoreditch, and we really encourage you to take advantage of that and get away from your desk during the day. There's a tonne of great coffee shops, lunch places and nice walks nearby, and we've put some of our favourites on a Google map. We'd love you to contribute too!

Tools we use

We use a bunch of different tools and integrations to make our workflow as smooth as possible. You should find emails about getting these set up in your inbox when you start, but if you think you're missing any then just grab your line manager.

We've popped a list of the most common tools we use over on Twine.

HR-y stuff

We're sure you'll have a bunch of questions about how things work here at Browser - what's the deal with the pension scheme? What do I do if I'm running late? When do we get paid?
